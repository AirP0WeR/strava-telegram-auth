Challenge 1:

1a. CycleToWork (No. of Rides)
- Needs registration at cycleto.work
- Rides have to be to the location registered on CycleToWork.
- Starting Bonus of 50 points each for the first 2 CycleToWork days (max points 100).
- Contribute your commutes to work for the #CycleToWork initiative by commuting 25 times to/from work.
- Accumulate 20 points for each CycleToWork rides (limited to a max of 500 points).
- Additional Bonus of 100 points if you have more than 15 CycleToWork days in October.

1b. CycleToWork (Distance Covered)
- Needs registration at cycleto.work
- Rides have to be to the location registered on CycleToWork.
- Starting Bonus of 50 points each for the first 2 CycleToWork days (max points 100).
- Contribute your commutes to work for the #CycleToWork initiative by commuting 500km to/from work.
- Accumulate 1 point for each CycleToWork rides by 1km (limited to a max of 500 points).
- Additional Bonus of 100 points if you reach 500 points first in this challenge.

Challenge 2:

2a. 2km x 30 (only for Woman riders)
- Do 30 rides of 2km each ride in the month of October 2019.
- It has to be single ride on Strava.
- Multiple rides adding up to 2km will not be counted.
- Multiple rides (of 2km or more) in a day are welcome and counted.
- Starting Bonus of 50 points each for the first 2 CycleToWork rides(max points 100).
- Accumulate 15 points for each counted ride(limited to first 30 rides. Max of 450 points).
- Additional Bonus of 50 points for completing the challenge(30 counted rides).
- Additional Bonus of 100 points for completing the challenge(35 counted rides).

2b: 40min x 30rides
- Do 30 rides of 40 minutes each ride in the month of October 2019.
- It has to be a single ride on Strava.
- Multiple rides adding up to 40 minutes will not be counted.
- Multiple rides (of 40 minutes or more) in a day are welcome and counted.
- Accumulate 20 points for each counted ride (limited to max of 600 points).
- Bonus of 50 points for completing the challenge(30 counted rides).
- Additional Bonus of 50 points for doing a 50+ km ride in October (Single ride).

2c: How far can you go
- Ride as much as you want/can in October.
- Multiple rides per day are welcome, however, do take the time to get off the saddle once in a while.
- Accumulate 1 points for every 1 km ridden (max 600 points).
- Additional Bonus of 50 points for doing a 150+ km ride in October (Single ride).
- Bonus of 50 points for completing 1000 km in October.
