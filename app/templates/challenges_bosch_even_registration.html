Challenge 1:

1. CycleToWork
- Contribute your commutes to work for the #CycleToWork initiative by commuting 10 times to/from work in June.
- Needs registration at cycleto.work
- Rides have to be to the location registered on CycleToWork.
- Starting Bonus of 50 points each for the first 2 CycleToWork days (max points 100).
- Accumulate 30 points for each CycleToWork ride including the first 2 rides (max 300 points).
- Additional Bonus of 100 points if you have more than 12 CycleToWork days in June.

Challenge 2:

2a: 6x15
- Do at least 15 rides of 6 km or more in the month of June, 2019.
- It has to be a single ride on Strava. Multiple rides adding up to 6 km in a day will not be counted.
- Multiple rides (of 6 or more km each) in a day are welcome and will be counted.
- Accumulate 6 points for each counted ride day (max 180 points).
- Bonus of 20 points for completing the challenge (15 counted ride days).
- Additional Bonus of 50 points for doing a 25+ km ride in June (single ride).

2b: 30x30
- Do 30 rides of 30 minutes each ride in the month of June, 2019.
- It has to be a single ride on Strava.
- Multiple rides adding up to 30 minutes will not be counted.
- Multiple rides (of 30 minutes or more) in a day are welcome and counted.
- Accumulate 6 points for each counted ride (limited to max of 180 points).
- Bonus of 20 points for completing the challenge (30 counted rides).
- Additional Bonus of 50 points for doing a 3+ hour ride in June (single ride).

2c: How far can you go
- Ride as much as you want/can in June.
- Multiple rides per day are welcome, however, do take the time to get off the saddle once in a while.
- Accumulate 6 points for every 30 kms ridden (max 180 points).
- Bonus of 20 points for completing 1000 kms in June.
- Additional Bonus of 50 points for doing a 150+ km ride in June (single ride).

General Conditions

- Challenge 1 is mandatory (riding 1 day to work gets you 160 points) - Only one of 2a, 2b, 2c can be selected (based on your comfort/experience level).
- Only outdoor rides recorded on Strava can be counted.
- Maximum 750 points can be accumulated from the challenges.
- Overall leaderboard will be based on total points accumulated from both the challenges.
