2 categories - Commuter / Distance
A: Commuter Monthly challenges
A1: Basic Commuter - 5 days or more of C2W
A2: Advance Commuter - 10 days or more of C2W
A3: Prime Commuter - 15 days or more of C2W

B: Endurance Cyclists Quarterly Challenges
Combination of base and bonus points for distance & elevation cycled
Base Points:
10 km = 2 points
100 meters = 1 point (Elevation gain)
1 activity = 2 points (Minimum 10 kms)

Bonus Points:
C2W Activity = 5 points (only 1 per day)
25 km in a single ride = 5 points
50 km in a single ride = 15 points
100 km in a single ride = 15 points

500 meters of elevation gain in a single ride = 10 points
1000 meters of elevation gain in a single ride = 25 points

3 consecutive 50s = 25 points
5 consecutive 50s = 50 points
2 consecutive 100s = 50 points
3 consecutive 100s = 100 points

Conditions:
A cap of 100 kms (single ride) is set for base points in Cycling. For eg. If a rider does a 150 km ride, he/she gets 20 base points only along with applicable bonus points.
Similarly, a cap of 2000 meters(single ride) of elevation gain is set for base points in Cycling. For eg. If a rider does a ride with 2500 meters of elevation gain, he/she gets 20 base points along with applicable bonus points.
