Base points:
10 km = 1 point
100 meters = 1 point (Elevation gain)
1 activity = 1 point (Minimum 30 mins or 10 kms)

Conditions:
A cap of 100 kms(single ride) is set for base points. For eg. If a rider does a 150 km ride, he gets 10 base points along with applicable bonus points.
Similarly, a cap of 1500 meters(single ride) of elevation gain is set for base points. For eg. If a rider does a ride with 2000 meters of elevation gain, he gets 15 points along with applicable bonus points.

Bonus points:
20 km in a single ride = 5 points
20 km for 5 successive days = 75 points
20 km for 20 days in a month = 150 points
20 km on all days of the month = 250 points

50 km in a single ride on a weekend = 10 points
50 km in a single ride on a weekday = 15 points
50 km for 5 successive days = 100 points*
50 km rides for 10 days in a month = 100 points**

100 km in a single ride on a weekend = 20 points
100 km in a single ride on a weekday = 30 points
100 km for 3 successive days = 100 points
100 km rides for 10 days in a month = 200

500 meters of elevation gain in a single ride = 10 points
1000 meters of elevation gain in a single ride = 25 points
10000 meters of elevation gain in a month = 150 points

1000 kms in a month = 150 points

Rides on first and last day of the month will fetch you 2x points.

*If a rider does 5 successive 50 km plus rides which has 3 successive 100 km rides, he gets the bonus points for 3 successive 100 km rides and not for 5 successive 50 km rides.
**Only rides between 50 km and 99.99 km are considered for this.

A single leader board will be published considering all the above parameters.

Every rider who reaches 600 points will get an e-certificate.
